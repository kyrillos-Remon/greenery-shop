Plant Care Made Easy

Follow these three golden rules to keep your plants thriving.

Water Wisely

Most plants prefer drying out between waterings. Stick your finger 2 inches into the soil; if dry, it's time to water.

Indirect Light

Bright, indirect sunlight is the sweet spot. Avoid direct midday sun to prevent leaf scorching.

Keep Warm

Indoor plants love stable temperatures between 18°C and 24°C. Avoid keeping them near AC vents or drafts.
